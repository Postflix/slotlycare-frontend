Program Terms — Operational Annex

1. Conversion Attribution

A conversion is attributed to the partner when the healthcare professional completes the SlotlyCare subscription using the partner's exclusive coupon or accessing through the dedicated landing page. Tracking is performed by two independent mechanisms.

  • Each subscription is attributed to a single partner.
  • In case of multiple coupons, the one used at payment prevails.
  • If a professional was in contact with two partners, the commission belongs exclusively to the partner whose link or coupon was used at the moment of purchase. There is no split attribution.
  • Subscriptions without a coupon or partner landing page do not generate commission.
  • SlotlyCare does not attribute conversions retroactively.

2. Reports

SlotlyCare generates a monthly report for each partner sent by the 10th of the following month, containing: subscriptions attributed, value, commission owed, and payment status. The partner has 15 calendar days to contest in writing.

3. Payments

Commission is paid within 30 days (D+30) after payment confirmation, respecting the 7-day guarantee window. In practice, the effective timeline is approximately D+45 from the purchase date. Payment method: international bank transfer or agreed platform.

4. Cancellations, Refunds & Chargebacks

  • Refund within 7 days: subscription cancelled, no commission owed.
  • After 7 days: SlotlyCare does not refund. Commission already paid remains valid.
  • Chargebacks: SlotlyCare reserves the right to deduct the corresponding value from future commissions or request refund from the partner.

5. Communication & Compliance

All communication to the partner's base is conducted by SlotlyCare with prior authorization. Every material is produced by SlotlyCare and validated by the partner before sending. The partner may not use the SlotlyCare brand for other purposes. SlotlyCare will not use the partner's name without express authorization.

6. Program Termination

Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. SlotlyCare may terminate immediately for conduct violations, brand misuse, spam or reputational damage. Commissions for conversions confirmed before termination remain due.

7. Launch Phase Conditions

The conditions — US$ 948.00 price, 30% partner discount, and 40% commission — are valid exclusively during the launch phase. SlotlyCare may end this phase at any time with 30 days notice to active partners.

8. General Provisions

  • Amendments must be communicated in writing and accepted by both parties.
  • In case of conflict, the Operational Annex prevails over the main document.
  • Unforeseeable matters will be resolved in good faith between the parties.
