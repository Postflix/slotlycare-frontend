Program Terms — Operational Annex

1. Conversion Attribution

A conversion is attributed to the partner when the healthcare professional completes the SlotlyCare subscription using the partner's exclusive coupon or accessing through the dedicated landing page.

Tracking is performed by two independent mechanisms: the exclusive coupon applied at the time of payment, and the partner's personalized landing page. Both are recorded automatically.

  • Each subscription is attributed to a single partner.
  • In case of multiple coupons, the one used at payment prevails.
  • Subscriptions without a coupon or partner landing page do not generate commission.
  • SlotlyCare does not attribute conversions retroactively.

2. Reports

SlotlyCare generates a monthly report for each partner, containing: number of subscriptions attributed in the period, value of each subscription, commission owed, and payment status.

The report is sent by the 10th of the following month. The partner has 15 calendar days after receipt to contest any information in writing. In the absence of contestation, the report is considered accepted.

3. Payments

Commission is paid within 30 days (D+30) after subscription payment confirmation, respecting the 7-day guarantee window. In practice, the effective payment timeline is approximately D+37 from the purchase date.

Payment method: international bank transfer or agreed payment platform.

4. Cancellations, Refunds & Chargebacks

  • Refund within guarantee period (7 days): subscription cancelled, no commission owed.
  • Cancellation after guarantee period: SlotlyCare does not refund after 7 days. Commission already paid remains valid.
  • Chargebacks: SlotlyCare reserves the right to deduct the corresponding value from future commissions or request refund from the partner.

5. Communication & Compliance

All communication to the partner's base is conducted by SlotlyCare, with prior partner authorization. Every material is produced by SlotlyCare and validated by the partner before sending.

The partner may not use the SlotlyCare brand, name or materials for purposes other than those established in the program. SlotlyCare will not use the partner's name or brand in public communications without express authorization.

The partner commits to not use spam, misleading communication, false promises or any approach that may harm SlotlyCare's reputation.

6. Program Termination

Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. SlotlyCare may terminate immediately in case of conduct violations, brand misuse, spam or reputational damage.

Upon termination, the coupon is deactivated and the landing page is removed. Commissions for conversions confirmed before termination remain due. Conversions after the termination date, even from earlier communications, do not generate commission.

7. Launch Phase Conditions

The conditions described — US$ 948.00 price, 30% partner discount, and 50% commission — are valid exclusively during SlotlyCare's launch phase. SlotlyCare may end this phase at any time with 30 days notice to active partners. Confirmed conversions maintain the conditions valid at the time of purchase.

8. General Provisions

  • Amendments must be communicated in writing and accepted by both parties.
  • In case of conflict, the Operational Annex prevails over the main document.
  • Unforeseeable matters will be resolved in good faith between the parties.
