Program Terms — SlotlyCare Partner Program

Version 2.0 — February 2026

What this document is

These terms describe how the SlotlyCare partner program works — how conversions are tracked, how commissions are calculated, and how payments are made. Everything here reflects exactly what is shown on this page. No surprises, no fine print.

1. How conversions are tracked

Every partnership comes with two independent tracking mechanisms: an exclusive coupon code and a dedicated landing page at slotlycare.com/yourorganization. A conversion is recorded when a healthcare professional completes a SlotlyCare subscription using your coupon.

Each subscription is attributed to a single partner. If a professional was in contact with more than one partner, the commission belongs exclusively to whoever's coupon was used at the moment of purchase. There is no split attribution. Subscriptions completed without a coupon do not generate commission.

2. Reports and transparency

SlotlyCare sends a monthly report to each partner by the 10th of the following month. The report includes: subscriptions attributed, subscription value, commission owed, and payment status. Every conversion is documented in Stripe — full traceability on every transaction.

3. Commissions and payments

The commission is of each subscription completed through your coupon — per conversion, based on the partner price of .

Payment is made within 30 days after the 7-day money-back guarantee window closes. In practice, this means approximately D+45 from the purchase date. Payment method: international bank transfer or mutually agreed platform.

If a refund is requested within the 7-day window, no commission is owed. After 7 days, the subscription is final and the commission remains valid regardless of any dispute.

4. Your brand and ours

SlotlyCare will never use your organization's name or brand in any public communication.

In return, the partner may not use the SlotlyCare brand, logo, or content for purposes outside this program without prior written authorization.

5. Launch phase conditions

The current conditions — product price, exclusive discount for your base, and commission — are valid during SlotlyCare's launch phase. They may change at any time, with 30 days notice to active partners. Conversions confirmed before any change keep their original conditions and payment is guaranteed.

6. Ending the partnership

Either party may end the partnership with 30 days notice. SlotlyCare may terminate immediately in cases of brand misuse, spam, or conduct that causes reputational damage. Commissions for conversions confirmed before termination remain due and will be paid normally.
